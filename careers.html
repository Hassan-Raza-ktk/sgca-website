Careers at SGCA

Join SGCA Teaching Staff

SGCA School System welcomes qualified, dedicated and responsible female teaching candidates who want to contribute to quality education in Village Chokara, Karak KPK.

Teaching Opportunities

We look for teachers who care about children and classroom discipline.

SGCA values teachers who can guide young students with patience, confidence and strong basic teaching skills. Interested candidates can share their education details, experience and CV with the school administration.

Who Can Apply?

Ideal candidate profile for teaching roles.

Female Candidates

Dedicated female teachers who can work with early years and primary students.

Relevant Education

Education in teaching, arts, science or related fields will be preferred.

Strong Basics

Good command of reading, writing, classwork and subject preparation.

Class Management

Ability to maintain discipline, attention and positive behavior in class.

Child Care

Patient, respectful and supportive attitude toward young learners.

Teaching Areas
