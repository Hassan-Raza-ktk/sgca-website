About SGCA

Rooted in Chokara, committed to confident learners.

Since 1995, SGCA School System has served Village Chokara, Karak KPK with disciplined education from PG to Class 7th.

School Profile

A trusted learning place for young students.

SGCA School System focuses on strong foundations, respectful behavior and regular classroom practice. The school supports children in their early and primary years so they can continue their education with confidence.

The school is managed by Administrator Muhammad Raza Khattak and supported by a dedicated female teaching staff.

Our Story

Serving local families with care and consistency.
